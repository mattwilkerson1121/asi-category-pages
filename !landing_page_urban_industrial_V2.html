fsc certified

Earth-centric and redesigned, our Rancho furniture line within our Urban Industrial collection is crafted from reclaimed wood-sourced from shipping pallets-and certified by the Forest Stewardship Council (FSC). The FSC ensures that products bearing its certification come from responsibly managed forests that are evaluated to meet strict environmental and social standards.

Certified companies in the marketplace sell FSC-certified wood, paper, and other forest products with the FSC label, so you can buy with confidence and peace of mind.
