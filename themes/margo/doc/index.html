Homepage variation

There are 8 types of Homepage variation in our bundle pack. You can use any one. Just rename the file name and write "index.html"

Topbar Variation

To change topbar color you need to add dark-bar or color-bar right after top-bar class.

Example: <div class="topbar dark-bar">

Example: <div class="topbar color-bar">

Map

In the contact page you can change your location map. For this you have to change latitude & longitude

In contact.html you will find data-position-latitude="30.044300" and you can change the value

and also you will find data-position-longitude="31.235700" and you can change the value.
